About Us

hackslash is an initiative to induce peer learning spirit among the students. it is built on the belief that learning together is pivotal to overall development. We want people to help others, and grow in the process. We aim to conduct bootcamps, coding contests, guest speaker sessions, workshops and many more exciting events! All you have to do to a part is to an avid learned and show your spirit of giving back to the community! So join the amazing journey in one of its kind club of GECA and experience a never before learning curve with your peers
